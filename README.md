## ⭐ Key Insights Discovered

Here are the most important churn insights uncovered through the analysis:

1. **Month-to-month contracts drive the highest churn.**  
   Customers on long-term contracts (1–2 years) show significantly lower churn.

2. **Customers with low tenure (< 12 months) churn the most.**  
   This indicates poor onboarding or low early engagement.

3. **Customers paying higher monthly charges tend to churn more.**  
   Suggests price sensitivity or dissatisfaction among high-paying users.

4. **Service engagement strongly reduces churn.**  
   Users subscribed to **5–7 services rarely churn**.

5. **AutoPay users churn significantly less.**  
   Auto-billing increases retention and reduces failed payments.

6. **Fiber optic internet users have higher churn rates.**  
   Indicates potential performance issues or unmet expectations.

7. **Users with fewer add-on services (security, backup, tech support) churn more.**  
   Add-on services increase stickiness and satisfaction.
